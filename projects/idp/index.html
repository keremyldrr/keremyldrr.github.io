Divergence-Free Shape Correspondence with Time Dependent Vector Fields

Posted: 2019-11-20

We are extending the paper “Divergence-Free Shape Correspondence by Deformation” and representing a motion sequence as a time dependent vector field. We solve correspondence and matching problems for the whole sequence during the optimization. Some of the current results can be seen below. Blue is our estimated deformation and orange is the ground truth in the Dynamic FAUST dataset.
